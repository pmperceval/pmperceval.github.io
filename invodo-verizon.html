Invodo / Verizon Wireless

Graphic Design / Corporate / Branding / Web Agency



Invodo is an agency based in Austin, Texas. I was brought on to work on two projects for Verizon Wireless. Not only was Verizon in the midst of a rebranding that we had to accomadate and build in cohesion with, but Verizon was also launching two new products: the Motorolla Bounce and LG V10. It was our job to create in store designs, interactive displays, and user interfaces for the instore phones, tablets, and kiosks that consumers would be interacting with upon launch.
